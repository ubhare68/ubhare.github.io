What is Stromectol ?

Stromectol is an anti-parasite medication. It causes the death of certain parasitic organisms in the body. Stromectol is used to treat infections caused by certain parasites. Stromectol is an anthelmintic. It works by killing sensitive parasites.



Use

This medication is used to treat certain parasitic roundworm infections. Curing parasitic infections helps to improve your quality of life. In people with weakened defense (immune) systems, curing roundworm infections can reduce the risk of developing a severe or life-threatening infection. This drug may also be used to treat other parasitic infections, including lice and scabies.
